Threat Hunting

What is Threat Hunting?

Threat hunting is the proactive and iterative process of searching for cyber threats within an organization's network. Unlike traditional cybersecurity measures that rely on predefined signatures and patterns, threat hunting involves actively looking for signs of malicious activity that may have gone unnoticed by traditional security tools. It aims to detect and mitigate threats before they cause significant damage.

Threat hunters use a combination of security expertise, data analysis, and cutting-edge tools to identify and respond to potential threats. This approach allows organizations to stay one step ahead of cyber attackers and safeguard their valuable data and assets.

By adopting a proactive threat hunting strategy, organizations can enhance their overall cybersecurity posture and reduce the risk of falling victim to advanced and sophisticated cyber threats.
